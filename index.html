The Process

Four steps to
movie night harmony

01
👥
Gather the Group
Enter 2 to 5 player names. Everyone votes on the same curated library of films — independently and privately.
02
🃏
Swipe the Deck
Each player gets their own shuffled deck. Swipe right for Yes, left to Skip, or up for Love. No one sees anyone else's votes.
03
⚖️
The Quorum Scores
A weighted algorithm tallies all votes in real time. Love counts triple. The film everyone wants rises to the top.
04
🏆
The Verdict
A cinematic reveal ceremony announces the winner with a full sentiment matrix showing exactly who liked what.

Why Quorum

Built for
real movie nights

🔒
State Partitioning
Each player votes in complete isolation. A handoff screen blanks the UI between turns — zero cross-contamination, zero influence bias.
⚡
Zero Backend
Everything runs in your browser. No accounts, no servers, no data sent anywhere. Open the file and play instantly.
🎬
Curated Library
17 hand-picked films across every genre — from Bollywood classics to Hollywood blockbusters. Something for everyone in the group.
📊
Sentiment Matrix
The results screen shows a full grid of who liked what — revealing hidden overlaps and sparking conversation about the runner-ups.
✨
Reveal Ceremony
A 5-second countdown, a cinematic podium, and confetti. Because movie night deserves a proper announcement.
📱
Touch Native
Built with Pointer Events API for buttery smooth kinetic swiping on any device — phone, tablet, or laptop trackpad.
The Algorithm

Weighted scoring
that makes sense

A plain vote count would treat a mild "yeah sure" the same as a passionate "I need to watch this tonight." Quorum doesn't.

Love votes carry triple weight, surfacing the films that people genuinely want — not just the ones nobody objected to.
